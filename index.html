    Hi, I'm Clint

    I'm a user experience designer with a hunger to learn and evolve my visual design skills. I enjoy researching and brainstorming to find the best possible creative solutions, with a focus on creating engaging UX copy.

    I have a curiosity for the human thought process and creative problem solving. Coming from a diverse background of experience from the music and customer service industries many of the skills learnt have transffered into my UX design process. Having developed an innate ability to listen and emphasize with people, I am able to understand and explore possible solutions through creative thinking.

    Although new to UX design, the years of experience in customer service roles has taught me the importance of strong communication and being present whilst listening to others speak. Working on UX projects, I find these transferrable skills to be valuable to defining user insights and problems.

    Passion for creative problem solving, I am driven by work thats allows me to contribute valuable solutions to the community. My goal is to create intuitive solutions that are based on fulfulling user's needs and desires.
